Lyte's Odyssey

Lyte's Odyssey is a educational video game, developed in Unity Engine as part of my bachelor's degree dissertation. The game attempts to utilize well established learning theories to educate children in the concept of abstraction, problem analysis and solving. While many of the final mechanics are common in video games, in this particular project they are based on a solid body of research. The player controls a buny that is tasked with saving the spirit tree, by traversing the land and reaching his goal. In his path he has to solve various puzzles, by utilizing both in-game and real life skill he aquires during the game.
