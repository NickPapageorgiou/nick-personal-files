Lyte's Odyssey

Lyte's Odyssey is a educational video game, developed in Unity Engine as part of my bachelor's degree dissertation. The game attempts to utilize well established learning theories to educate children in the concept of abstraction, problem analysis and solving. While many of the final mechanics are common in video games, in this particular project they are based on a solid body of research. The player controls a buny that is tasked with saving the spirit tree, by traversing the land and reaching his goal. In his path he has to solve various puzzles, by utilizing both in-game and real life skill he aquires during the game.

Rainbow Skyway

Rainbow Skyway is a fast paced arcade shooter game, where you control a flying whale fighting against the evil "gray ones". Seven unique powerups based on the colours of the rainbow, six unique bosses all with their own special powers and voice lines. Developed in Game Maker 8.

The Human Constructor

The Human Constructor is a procedurally generated dungeon turn based dungeon crawler. Navigate through the random dungeons, deeper towards the creator. Battle all kinds of enemies, gain levels, upgrade your skills, draft various passive and active abilites for your robot. Developed from the ground up using only the core JAVA libraries and a text editor, as a challenge to myself.
